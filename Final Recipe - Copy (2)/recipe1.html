Banana Pancakes

A pancake is a thin, flat, circular piece of cooked batter made from milk, flour, and eggs. Pancakes are often rolled up or folded and eaten hot with a sweet or savoury filling inside. In America, pancakes are usually eaten for breakfast, with butter and maple syrup. Out of all fruit infused pancakes, banana pancakes are most popular and widely appreciated one. The mashed banana mixed with routine pancake mix adds a nice flavor and taste to it. This recipe prepares it from scratch and requires the use of cooktop or griddle.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : beef breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Sift wheat flour in a large bowl. Add baking powder, sugar and salt.

step 2

Mix well and Choose the very ripe banana. Peel the banana, cut it into slices and place in a small bowl, then Mash it with a fork until smooth puree.

step 3

Add milk, curd, vanilla extract, 1 tablespoon butter and a pinch of cardamom powder (or ground cinnamon). Whisk all together to make a mixture of wet ingredients, then Pour wet ingredients mixture over flour mixture (prepared in step-2) and Stir gently until there are no lumps of dry flour.

step 4

If the batter looks very thick, add 1-2 tablespoon more milk and mix again. Prepared batter should be slight lumpy. For fluffy pancake, do not over-beat the mixture, and Heat a non-stick flat frying pan over medium high heat. When pan is medium hot, add 1/2 teaspoon of butter and pour 1/4 cup batter onto it for each pancake. Reduce heat to low and cook for around 2 minutes until bubbles appear on top surface and underside is golden brown.

step 5

Flip it and cook other side until golden brown. Second side will take less time to cook than first side, then Transfer banana pancakes to a serving plate and sprinkle chopped cashew nuts (or almonds) over it. Serve it hot with maple syrup and sliced banana.

ingredients

1/2 cup Whole Wheat Flour / All Purpose Flour

1/2 teaspoon Baking Powder

1 teaspoon Sugar

A Pinch of Salt

1/4 cup mashed Ripe Banana (approx. 1 medium)

1/2 cup Milk

1 tablespoon Curd

2-3 drops of Vanilla Extract

A Pinch of Cardamom Powder or Ground Cinnamon

1 tablespoon Butter + for greasing

Maple Syrup, for serving

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
